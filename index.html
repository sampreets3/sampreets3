Hello, my name is Sampreet Sarkar. I recently graduated from Ecole Centrale de Nantes, where I was pursuing my Master's in Control and Robotics, specialising in Embedded Real-Time Systems.
I did my end-of-studies internship in the domain of Embedded Vision and Machine Learning at 3D Aerospace, a young European start-up working in AI-Enabled predictive maintenance for the Viticulture industry. My work was focussed in using Deep Learning and Computer Vision to detect and track individual vines in a vineyard. It was a great experience for me since I not only got to learn about how we can use Computer Vision to our advantage in the field, but was also able to integrate the concepts of viticulture that I gained from my colleagues into the solution.
