Hello, my name is Sampreet Sarkar. I recently graduated from Ecole Centrale de Nantes, where I was pursuing my Master's in Control and Robotics, specialising in Embedded Real-Time Systems.

I did my end-of-studies internship in the domain of Embedded Vision and Machine Learning at 3D Aerospace, a young European start-up working in AI-Enabled predictive maintenance for the Viticulture industry. My work was focussed in using Deep Learning and Computer Vision to detect and track individual vines in a vineyard. It was a great experience for me since I not only got to learn about how we can use Computer Vision to our advantage in the field, but was also able to integrate the concepts of viticulture that I gained from my colleagues into the solution.

I am currently working as a research engineer at the French Atomic Energy Commission (Commissariat à l'énergie atomique et aux énergies alternatives)a in Nantes, where I work in the Robotics for the Assistance of Humans team. My current work focusses on the control and path planning for a collaborative robot arm to reach objects in a rapidly changing environment. It's a fun project, which has helped me learn a lot about the real-world applications for robotics. Using ROS2 and MoveIt2 to generate a plan to an object in a scene also gave me a decent bit of exposure towards the overall software development side of things.

Since my name is not Jack and I am not a dull boy, I think it is safe to assume that my life is "some work, and some play". I am interested in a lot of things and curious by nature, so I generally try to take up a wide variety of activities. Not all of them stick with me long-term, it's still a process of trial and error for me. Some things I do like to do in my free time is hang out with my friends (We play darts a lot), playing the guitar/modifying guitar effects pedals (yes I know, super nerdy stuff), and if the weather permits, go for a nice walk to de-stress and relax a bit.
